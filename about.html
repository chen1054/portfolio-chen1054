Hello, I’m Xinyan Chen

Before I study UX design, I worked as an interior designer for 5 years.

I enjoyed creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces that aligned with clients’ needs. This experience sharpened my ability to understand user behaviour, problem-solve, and design with purpose.

My transition to UX/UI design began when I realized my passion for human-centered design and digital experiences. Now, as an Interactive Media Design student, I am building expertise in user research, wire-framing, and prototyping, while applying my design thinking skills in new ways.

Why UX/UI design?

UX/UI design is about crafting intuitive and meaningful experiences. My background in interior design trained me to think critically about how people interact with spaces, and now I apply that same mindset to digital products— ensuring usability, accessibility, and delightful interactions.Moving forward, I hope to collaborate with designers and developers to build seamless, user-friendly digital experiences that make an impact.
